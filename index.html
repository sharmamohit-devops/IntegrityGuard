How IntegrityGuard Works

Our six-pillar approach ensures comprehensive security and fairness in virtual assessments and interviews

1

Pre-Session Verification

Candidate identity is verified using multi-factor authentication, including facial recognition, ID document verification, and liveness detection to prevent impersonation.

2

Secure Environment Setup

Our system locks down the testing environment by disabling unauthorized software, secondary displays, and external devices, ensuring a controlled assessment space.

3

Continuous AI Monitoring

Advanced AI algorithms monitor audio, video, and screen activity in real-time, flagging suspicious behaviors like unusual eye movements or external voices.

4

Real-Time Incident Alerts

Instant notifications and detailed incident logs alert proctors to potential violations, allowing for immediate intervention if necessary.

5

Secure Live Interviews

Conduct live video call interviews with secure chat and note-taking, all integrated with AI monitoring for real-time integrity checks.

6

Comprehensive Reporting

Post-session, recruiters receive a detailed report with trust scores, video evidence, chat logs, notes, and timestamps of flagged incidents for transparent decision-making.
